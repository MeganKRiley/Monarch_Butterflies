Of course, unless, there is a murder in Mexico.
A Brief Education
          Every year, Monarch butterflies migrate from the norther part of the use to Mexico by means of any mode possible. They fly, ride on boats down river, or glide with a favorable wind. They overwinter down in Mexico, then spawn on their way back, however it takes two to three generations to get back north for another batch to make the trip back down.
